Travel to Gradignan

Why Gradignan ?

  • Beautiful landscapes
  • Mysterious lore
  • Just the right amount of dangerousness to feel alive(maybe)

Is it safe to live ?

The atmosphere is very tense but not unsafe. You can walk in the streets without concerns even at night. Just be careful of angry spirits wandering at the "Village 4" bus stop.
